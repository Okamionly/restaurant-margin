## 🎯 Réponses prêtes aux commentaires PH typiques

### "What about Toast / Square / Lightspeed?"
```
Great question! Toast/Square are POS systems — they help you take orders and process payments.

RestauMargin tackles a different problem: BEFORE the order is taken, what should you be charging? What's actually profitable?

We sit upstream: recipe costing → menu engineering → margin tracking. Most of our users have a POS already and use RestauMargin alongside.

Open to integrate with Toast/Square in the future if there's demand 🙌
```

### "Why French market only?"
```
Solid question! The French restaurant industry has very specific needs:
- TVA at 10% (vs 5.5% for groceries) requires specific accounting
- Mandatory food origin labeling
- Specific allergen rules
- Strong artisanal restaurant culture (vs chain-dominated US market)

Building deep > broad. Once we nail it for French independents, expansion is on the roadmap.
```

### "Is voice dictation reliable for recipes?"
```
Honest answer: it's at 95% accuracy for common ingredients in French. Edge cases (regional names, very specific cuts of meat) sometimes need a manual touch.

We use Claude Haiku for the parsing — it understands "boeuf bourguignon" as a real recipe, not a translation.

Free 7-day trial — best way to test it on YOUR recipes 🍴
```

### "How is it different from a spreadsheet?"
```
Three things that Excel can't do:
1. Auto-update when supplier prices change (you'd manually re-enter everything)
2. Voice dictation in 10s vs 5min typing
3. Boston Matrix menu engineering (Stars/Puzzles/Plowhorses/Dogs auto-classification)

Plus the obvious: Excel breaks. RestauMargin syncs across web, tablet (kiosk mode), and phone.
```
